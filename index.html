welcome!

I'm a writer based in Hamilton, ON. I have over 11 years' experience writing for a variety of industries, including SaaS, cannabis, home appliances, visual art, hospitality, and retail.

These days I focus mostly on cannabis. Since 2017 I've consulted with medical clinics and Licensed Producers on creating both internal and external content, and I've been a regular contributor at the Ontario Cannabis Store since 2021.

In addition to being a writer, I'm also a web developer. These two skills have led me to UX writing. I think a lot about clear instructions, and I want to make the web a more inclusive and accessible place.
